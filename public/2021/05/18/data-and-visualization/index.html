Mass vs weight

we will study the mass vs weight relationship through a scatterplot

library(ggplot2)
ggplot(starwars,aes(x=height, y= mass))+geom_point()+labs(title = "Mass vs. height of Starwars characters",
       x = "Height (cm)", y = "Weight (kg)")

library(Tmisc)
quartet %>%
  group_by(set) %>%
  summarise(
    mean_x = mean(x), 
    mean_y = mean(y),
    sd_x = sd(x),
    sd_y = sd(y),
    r = cor(x, y)
  )
