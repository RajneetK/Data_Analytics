Goodreads Book

Description:

The dataset is from kaggle website about Goodreads book.

  1. Dataset information

    Goodreads offers a platform for people interested in talking about books.Analyze book data not only to understand users reading habits, but also to predict what books are worth reading.

    All books on GoodRead books are included in this data sets.

    The dataset includes 10 columns, including the books and the scores and reviews.

  2. The objective is to predict the rating of the book and the analysis which most affects the evaluation.
